About Us

Welcome to Anand Surgical Hospital Pvt.Ltd.

Anand surgical hospital pvt. ltd is an endeavor to alleviate patients suffering by providing the best of healthcare at an optimal cost. anand surgical hospital is a state-of-art comprehensive, multispeciality tertiary care hospital. Anand surgical hospital is a 150 bedded hospital that is located near memco cross roads naroda Ahmedabad adorned with state of art infrastructure and an eminent panel of doctors.

A truly sophisticated set up and a panel of super specialists in a culture of care and commitment, dedication and concern is what we mutually bring out. Our team believes in and work towards a cause and tirelessly strives to understand the patient, his physical and mental suffering. we are committed to excellence and giving the patients and attendants, speedy recovery.
